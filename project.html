Doctor patient portal

My Project Domain is Doctor Patient Portal.It is about the online appointment with the doctor. Normally we will take appointments through a phone call or by going to Hospital. Here we can take appointment through online in our free time.We will select the required specialist from our surrounding hospitals and make an appointment.There are also other modules like organ donars,blood donars.when we go to doctor and for the next visit the prescription is stored in the database.No need to take prescription with us. Software Languages used :Html ,php,Mysql. Advantages:No more waiting in the Clinic. No Boudaries. Medical history is stored in the online.
